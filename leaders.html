Leading Healthy Teams

The Aim

Leaders must adapt their management style to address the challenges associated with new waysof working.
Developing a new performance mindset starts with exploring what hybrid leadership is and how itdiffers from traditional face-face leadership in the context of an organisation's vision and strategy.This approach explores the direct relationship between a team’s purpose and performance to createand maintain a healthy team.
Consideration should also be given to the impact of attitudes, beliefs, and behaviours towardsleading others and delivering results in hybrid teams. This includes an exploration of self-awarenessand relationship intelligence. So too, an understanding of wellbeing needs, the development ofstrategies for promoting self-leadership in the team, and a step-by-step process for managingperformance.
